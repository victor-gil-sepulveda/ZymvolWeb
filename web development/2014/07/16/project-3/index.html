ZYMVOL enhances laboratory protein evolution through fast and reliable computer simulations

What

we do

Protein design

Accelerated protein optimization and cost reduction thanks to in silico protein evolution.
At ZYMVOL scientists combine expertise with special purpose software to provide customers with effective smart mutant libraries.

Cloud-based software

Our cloud-based platform allows scientists at ZYMVOL to predict the effect of thousands of mutations in days.
On-demand cloud-computing renders HPC possible without high initial investment and maintenance. We offer the same competitive advantage to our customers by migrating their favorite molecular modeling tools.

B2B

We close the gap between traditional Chemical Industry and protein producers.
ZYMVOL will diagnose potential bio-processes and find the right partners for implementation.

How

we do it

Computer simulations

We use state-of-the-art molecular modeling to provide atomic level detail of your favorite protein system. This in silico microscope reveals fundamental dynamic information not accessible with experimental characterization.

Screening of thousands of mutations

Thanks to computer simulation thousands of mutations can be tested in a fraction of time and cost of experimental procedures.

Third party technologies

Scientists at ZYMVOL have experience and expertise with a wide range of molecular modeling software. We use the most appropriate tools to meet the customer’s needs.
